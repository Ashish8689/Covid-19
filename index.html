How to Protect Yourself ?

Prevention and Advice

There is currently no vaccine to prevent Coronvirus(Covid-19). The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed to the virus.

Soap on hand

Regularly or throughly clean your hand with an alcohol based sanitizer or wash them with soap and water atleast for 20 second.

Maintain social distancing

Maintain atleast 1 meter (3 feet) of distance from person who are coughing and sneezing as it get a chance that you can be affected to.

Avoid touching face

Hand can touch many surface who probably have virus . So hand can transfer the virus by your nose,mouth,eyes if you touch any of them with your hand.

Practice respiratory hygiene

Maintain good respiratory hygiene as covering your Mouth and Nose with bent elbow or tissue with cough or sneeze.
